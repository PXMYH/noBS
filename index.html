So in a broom closet in his San Fernando valley home, along with his friend Kimberley Esliza, Borromeo created Wondery, one of the only outdoor apparel brands dedicated exclusively to women, particularly women of color. “A lot of my core memories and emotions stem from the outdoors,” Borromeo said, noting that one of his fondest experiences was taking his dog, Luca, for a swim in the Kern River.
